H_ART THE BAND

H_art the band is made up of three members. Mordecai Mwinyi who is the lead singer, Wachira Gatama who is the poet and Kenneth Muya, the guitarist. The journey to the top was no walk in the park. They were set book actors, except Mordecai, at the Kenya National Theatre and performed in other small plays. Although they earned a few coins, they felt they needed to switch to music. At first the plan was for Mordecai to sing as Kenneth plays the piano but they decided to bring Wachira, who is more of a poet, on board and theyâ€™ve never looked back. The three became a team when they auditioned for a cancer awareness play at the Kenya National Theatre late in 2012.In 2013 they sang a political song and thereafter came up with their name H_art the band.They signed a deal with Penya records and were enrolled in Sauti Sol Academy. Little by little, they grew as a band and started performing in gigs.Their lyrical content, delivery and stage presence is what should define Kenyan music. Those who have attended H_art the Band shows will agree with me that Kenya overflows with talent. The trio style is refreshingly unique and so is their dress code thus making them to be my favourite.
